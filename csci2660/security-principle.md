# Principle

## Security
- The prevention of, or protect against
    - access to information by unauthorized recipient
    - intentional but unauthorized destruction or alternation of that information
- The protection of information systems from
    - theft or damnage to the hardware, software

__Security Property__
- C-I-A trial
    - Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability
- (Some) other properties
    - Authentication / Authenticity
    - Authorization / Appropritate yse
    - Non-repudiation / Accountability / Auditability
    - Anonymity

__C-I-A trial__
- Captures the three fundamental properties that make any system valueable
- Confidentiality
    - An assest is viewed only by authorized parties
- Integrity
    - An asset is modified only by authorized parities
- Availability
    - An asset can be used by any autohrized parities
- Authenticity
    - The abiltiy to determine that statements, policies, and permissions issued by persons or systems are genuine
- Anonymity
    - The property that certain records/trancations cannot be attributed to any individual
